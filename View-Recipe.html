This Mediterranean quinoa salad is packed with fresh vegetables, herbs, and a zesty lemon dressing. It's perfect for meal prep, picnics, or as a healthy side dish. High in protein and fiber, it's both delicious and nutritious.

ğŸ›’ Ingredients

For 4 servings

  • Quinoa 1 cup
    Rinsed well
  • Cucumber 1 piece
    Diced
  • Cherry Tomatoes 1 cup
    Halved
  • Red Onion 1/2 piece
    Finely chopped
  • Kalamata Olives 1/2 cup
    Pitted and halved
  • Feta Cheese 100g
    Crumbled
  • Fresh Parsley 1/4 cup
    Chopped
  • Lemon Juice 3 tbsp

ğŸ“ Note: This recipe serves 4 people. Can be stored in fridge for 3 days.

ğŸ“ Instructions

1

Cook the Quinoa

Rinse quinoa thoroughly under cold water. Cook according to package directions (usually 1 cup quinoa to 2 cups water). Let cool completely.

ğŸ’¡ Tip: For extra flavor, cook quinoa in vegetable broth instead of water.
2

Prepare Vegetables

Chop cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and parsley into bite-sized pieces. Pit and halve the olives.

3

Make the Dressing

In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until emulsified.

4

Combine Ingredients

In a large bowl, combine cooled quinoa, chopped vegetables, olives, and crumbled feta cheese. Pour dressing over and toss gently to combine.

5

Chill and Serve

Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow flavors to meld. Garnish with extra parsley before serving.
